## Implementation Tips

### Start Small
Begin with these essentials:
1. Emergency contacts
2. Community directory
3. Skills list
4. Tool library
5. Meeting schedule

### Grow Organically
Add sections as needs arise:

- Power outage happens â†’ Add energy system docs
- New member joins â†’ Add onboarding info

### Keep It Current
- Assign someone to update monthly
- Note last update date on each page
- Remove outdated information
- Archive old versions if needed

### Make It Accessible
- Use simple language
- Include visual diagrams/maps
- Offer information in multiple formats
- Consider translation for multilingual communities

## Questions to Guide Your Content

**Ask your community:**
1. What information do people ask for most often?
2. What causes confusion or conflict?
3. What do new members need to know?
4. What would be critical in an emergency?
5. What knowledge is at risk of being lost?
6. What would help people connect and share?
7. What makes your community special?

**The answers will tell you what content to prioritize.**
